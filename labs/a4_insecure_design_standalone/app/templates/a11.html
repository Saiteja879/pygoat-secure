Insecure Design (OWASP A04:2021)

What is Insecure Design?

Insecure design represents missing or ineffective controls in an application. A secure implementation cannot fix a broken design. This lab demonstrates how flawed logic in user identity or access control can lead to business logic abuse.

You can claim up to 5 free movie tickets. But is that limit enforced securely? Can you trick the system into giving you more?

    Hint: Try logging out, logging back in, or reusing tickets.
Access Lab

Mitigation Tips

  • Use proper session tracking and identity enforcement
  • Validate ticket ownership server-side
  • Use threat modeling to detect design flaws
  • Limit actions per user/session using signed tokens or backend checks
